Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs Press Conferences Corpus (CMFA PressCon v6)

These data are from the Harvard Dataverse repository. They were originally produced
and published as part of the following research paper:

  Mochtak, M. & Turcsanyi, R.Q. (2021). Studying China's foreign policy rhetoric:
  Introducing the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs Press Conferences Corpus.
  Journal of Chinese Political Science.
  https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s11366-021-09762-3

Data repository (Harvard Dataverse):
  https://dataverse.harvard.edu/dataset.xhtml?persistentId=doi:10.7910/DVN/BAKGET

The dataset contains 35,346 question-and-answer pairs from regular press conferences
held by the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Affairs, covering the period from October 2002
to December 2025.

Retrieved by Haohan Chen on April 12, 2026 for teaching purpose in POLI3148 Data
